How to use-

  • Set the minutes(below 60) and seconds(below 60) first.
  • Click on Start to start the stopwatch.
  • Click on Pause to pause the stopwatch.
  • Click on Stop to stop and make stopwatch at starting position.
  • Click on Restart to restart the stopwatch.
  • Free Stopwatch doesn't need value to be entered.
  • Reset will reset the values in text area.
  • Use Reset for settingevery new timer
